🛡️ Pension Deduction Rules

Are CPP & EI deducted from passive pension income?

No. Passive Pension Income is 100% Exempt:

CPP and EI premiums are never deducted from passive retirement income, including CPP retirement benefits, OAS, GIS, or private/corporate pension payouts.

If You Choose to Work While Collecting a Pension:

CPP (Age 60–65): Deductions from employment earnings are mandatory and build a Post-Retirement Benefit (PRB).
CPP (Age 65–70): Voluntary. Opt-out by filing CRA Form CPT30 with your employer.
CPP (Age 70+): Contributions cease. No deductions allowed on any earnings.
EI (No Age Limit): EI premiums must be deducted from insurable employment income regardless of age.
